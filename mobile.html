Process Overview

Guardian Billing Automation Flow

End-to-end process for identifying, analyzing, and generating billing codes for a practice's patient census.
1
Input

Patient Census Import

The practice's active patient list is loaded into the system. Each patient record includes demographics, active diagnoses, insurance status (Medicare / QMB), current enrollment in home health or hospice agencies, and consent documentation for care management programs.

Patient demographics
Name · DOB · MBI
Active diagnoses

Enrollment status
HH · Hospice · QMB
2
Eligibility Check

Program Eligibility Screening

Every patient is evaluated against all applicable programs simultaneously. Home health or hospice enrollment does not lock a patient into a single path. Each eligible program is calculated independently, and the highest-value combination is selected. Concurrent billing conflicts are enforced, but profitability always drives the final recommendation.

APCM Eligible

Any Medicare patient. Depending on chronic condition count and QMB status. No time requirement. Requires population health management platform.

CCM Eligible

2+ chronic conditions. Routes to staff track (99490) or provider track (99491) based on who documents time. Compared against APCM for best value.

Home Health Oversight

Active HH enrollment adds cert/recert codes plus monthly supervision if physician time ≥30 min. Evaluated alongside and compared to APCM/CCM.

Hospice Oversight

Active hospice enrollment adds the hospice oversight code if physician time ≥30 min. Still compared against APCM/CCM. The highest value wins.

3
Time Review

Time Spent Verification

For each eligible patient, the system pulls documented time from the EHR, split by provider time (physician/NP personally performed) and clinical staff time (supervised by physician). These are counted separately and never pooled, as they drive different billing tracks. Home health oversight time is tracked independently as physician supervision minutes.

Provider Time (drMin)
Personally performed by physician or NP/PA. Qualifies for 99491 (≥30 min) and 99437 add-ons. Also used for G0181/G0182 oversight thresholds.
Staff Time (staffMin)
Performed by clinical staff under physician supervision. Qualifies for 99490 (≥20 min) and 99439 add-ons. Cannot be counted toward provider-track codes.
4
Algorithm

Optimal Code Calculation

Every patient is run through all eligible programs regardless of home health or hospice enrollment. HHO and hospice oversight codes are calculated and added where applicable, then compared against APCM and CCM to determine the highest-value billable combination. A QMB patient on home health, for example, may yield more under APCM G0558 than under HHO — the algorithm finds it either way.

calculate HHO value (if enrolled) + Hospice Oversight value (if enrolled)  |  calculate max(APCM, CCM_staff, CCM_provider)  |  bill highest compliant combination
5
Output

Billing Notes & Code Generation

For each patient, the system generates a structured billing note formatted to CMS documentation standards — including consent verification, active conditions, time log, care coordination activities, and the attached billing codes. Notes are ready for EHR import or direct claim submission.

Billing Code List

Patient-level code assignments with reimbursement amounts, ready for claim submission.

EHR Billing Note

Structured care management note per patient with all required CMS documentation elements.

Revenue Summary

Total estimated monthly reimbursement across the census with program distribution breakdown.

Key Billing Rules Enforced by the Algorithm APCM (G0556/G0557/G0558) and CCM (99490–99437) are mutually exclusive in the same calendar month. Staff-track CCM (99490/99439) and provider-track CCM (99491/99437) cannot be billed together. Home Health Oversight and Hospice Oversight cannot be billed together. HHO excludes APCM and CCM; Hospice Oversight excludes APCM and CCM — but in all cases, the algorithm evaluates every eligible program and selects the highest-value compliant combination before applying exclusions. Rates reflect 2025 Medicare Physician Fee Schedule national averages.
